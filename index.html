Omar El-Sharif

(1932 - 2015)

Omar Sharif was an Egyptian actor who gained international fame, starting his career in Egyptian cinema before moving to Hollywood. He became widely known for his roles in films such as Lawrence of Arabia (1962) and Doctor Zhivago (1965). He won several global awards and was one of the most prominent cinematic figures representing Egypt worldwide.

Umm Kulthum

(1898 - 1975)

Umm Kulthum was an iconic Arab singer and one of the most famous voices in the world. Her career spanned over five decades, and she is known for timeless songs such as Enta Omri and Al-Atlal. She was nicknamed the "Star of the East" and achieved widespread fame, reaching audiences in Europe and the United States, making her a cultural symbol of Egypt.
